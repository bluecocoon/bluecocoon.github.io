Features

Experiential Process

Students will learn by experience instead of informative lectures. Professionals will guide the whole learning process and allow students to explore many possibilities.

Application Based

It is important to know ‘the context for the content’ to make content relevant. Skills learned in the classroom are immediately applied

Project Driven

Entire learning will be through developing the projects to understand the application of subjects thought in college curriculum.









How It Works

Blue Cocoon brings experts from industry to the campus so as to provide application based experiential training to students to augment their existing curriculum.
The aim is to develop technical and other soft skills for the holistic growth of students during their academic years and empower them for a bright future.



Awareness Phase

  • Motivating students for practical and application based learning.
  • Delivered by organising Seminars, Workshops, Competitions and other such events.

Experiential Learning Phase

  • Implement a 5-semester project based practical training programme that goes hand in hand with the curriculum. It complements the subjects students learn during their academic years.
  • The programme focuses on the fundamental knowledge and skills. It involves current generation technologies used in professional and enterprise applications.
  • Experienced and knowledgeable professionals are to deliver the programme.
